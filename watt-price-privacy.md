# Privacy Policy for Watt Price

**Effective Date:** 20 March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **Watt Price** (the "Application") handles information. The Application is developed by Richard Russell ("Service Provider") and is provided to you "AS IS."

---

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

The Application is designed to function without the collection of personal user data.

- **No Personal Information:** The Application does not request, collect, or transmit personally identifiable information (PII), such as names, email addresses, or account credentials.
- **No Tracking or Analytics:** The Application does not include third-party tracking SDKs, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics.
- **Local Configuration:** Any user-defined settings (such as your energy region or selected tariff code) are stored locally on your Apple Watch. This data is not uploaded to any external servers.

## 2. External API Requests

To provide live pricing information, the Application makes outbound network requests to the **Octopus Energy REST API**.

- **Standard Metadata:** As with any internet-based request, these calls involve the transmission of the device’s IP address to the API provider (Octopus Energy). We do not log, store, or have access to this address ourselves.
- **Lawful Basis (GDPR/UK GDPR):** Under European and UK data protection laws, the transmission of this metadata is processed based on _Contractual Necessity_. It is technically required to deliver the specific pricing data you have requested.
- **Encryption:** All communication between the Application and the API is conducted over encrypted channels (HTTPS/TLS) to protect data in transit.
- **No Identity Sharing:** No user-specific identifiers, account tokens, or private keys are included in these requests.

## 3. Device Permissions

- **Location:** The Application does not request or use your GPS or physical location.
- **Sensors:** The Application does not access Apple Watch hardware sensors or HealthKit data.

## 4. Your Privacy Rights

While the Application does not collect personal data, we respect global privacy standards (such as GDPR and CCPA/CPRA). Because we do not store your data or link IP addresses to identities, we generally have no personal records to provide or delete. However, if you have any questions about your privacy rights, you are welcome to reach out.

## 5. Children’s Privacy

The Application does not collect any personal data from any user. Therefore, it does not knowingly or intentionally collect information from children under the age of 13.

## 6. Security

The Service Provider prioritizes the security of the Application by minimizing the data surface area. By design, the Application does not store or transmit sensitive personal data, which significantly reduces the risk of such information being compromised.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Users are responsible for maintaining the physical and digital security of their Apple Watch (e.g., using a device passcode) to protect any configuration data stored locally.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

The Service Provider may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon being updated on this page. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically.

## 8. Data Controller

For the purpose of data protection laws, Richard Russell acts as the Data Controller for this application.
